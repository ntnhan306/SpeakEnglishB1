    PHẦN 3 – Topic development

    Topic: Examinations are not necessary

    => In my opinion, sometimes examinations are not necessary for these reasons:

    - First, exams cost a lot of money for both schools and students.

    - Second, preparing for exams takes a lot of time from both teachers and students.

    - Third, it is not fair to judge a person’s ability by one exam. Sometimes, students feel tired or nervous.

    - Finally, exams can cause stress and affect students’ health.

    To conclude, I think exams are not always necessary because they waste time and money and are not always fair.

    Follow – up questions

    1. Do you think schools are necessary? Why? If not, why not?

    Yes, I do think schools are necessary. Because schools help us learn important knowledge. Also, going to school lets us meet new friends and practice talking with others. This helps us build good social skills.

    2. How do modern technologies change the way people study?

    In the past, people only learned in classrooms. But now, with a computer or smartphone and internet, people can study anytime and anywhere. They can learn at home, in a café, or while traveling. This makes learning easier and more flexible.

    3. Do you think that traditional schools will disappear in the future because of the development of modern technology?

    I don’t think traditional schools will disappear. Technology will help teachers and students but cannot replace them completely. The teachers and students need to talk and work together. Technology is a good tool to support learning, not to take the place of teachers.

    PHẦN 3 – Topic development

    Topic: Smoking should be banned in public places.

    In my opinion, smoking should be banned in public places for some reasons:

    - First, smoking is harmful to people’s health. It can cause lung problems and cancer after a long time.

    - Second, smoking affects people near the smoker, called second-hand smokers. For example, babies and women can get sick if they breathe smoke from their family members. Smoking during pregnancy can harm the baby.

    - Third, smokers spend a lot of money on cigarettes. This can cause stress and make their life harder.

    - Finally, cigarette butts pollute the environment because they are hard to break down. Smoking can also cause fires.

    - Smoking also sets a bad example for children who might try to copy it.

    Because of these reasons, I think smoking should be banned in public places like schools, hospitals, and bus stations.

    Follow-up questions:

    Do you smoke?

    No, I don’t. / Yes, I do.

    Why do many people want to smoke?

    - People smoke to relax and reduce stress.

    - Some smoke for fun.

    - Others think smoking helps them focus, get new ideas, or seem more grown-up.

    What should be done to prevent smoking in public places?

    I think the government should:

    - Put heavy fines on people who smoke in public.

    - Help people know the bad effects of smoking.

    - Put “No Smoking” signs in public places.

    PHẦN 3 – Topic development

    Topic: Things that affect people’s job satisfaction

    In my opinion, job satisfaction depends on several key factors.

    - Firstly, qualifications help decide the right position and salary, which affects satisfaction. For example, someone with higher qualifications usually gets a better-paid job that meets their expectations.

    - Secondly, a positive attitude at work makes people enjoy their jobs more. If employees feel confident and motivated, they are likely to be more productive and happier.

    - Thirdly, good relationships with colleagues create a friendly environment and improve teamwork. This can make daily work more enjoyable and reduce stress.

    - Finally, comfortable and safe working conditions help employees focus better and feel valued.

    In short, all these factors together influence how satisfied people are with their jobs.

    Follow-up questions:

    Do you think companies should promote experienced workers?

    In my opinion, promotion should be based on excellent performance and completing tasks well, not only on experience.

    Do you think workers should change companies often? Why/Why not?

    Yes, but not too often. Changing jobs can help workers gain new skills, preparing them better for future positions.

    What motivates workers more: salary or working conditions?

    I think most workers prefer salary because it provides financial security. However, good working conditions are also important for long-term happiness.
